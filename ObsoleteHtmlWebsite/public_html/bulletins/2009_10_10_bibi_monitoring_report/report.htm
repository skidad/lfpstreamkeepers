Saturday October 10 2009 BIBI Monitoring Report

October 26, 2009

We had a good turnout of over 15 people for the Fall 2009 Benthic Inventory of Biological Integrity (BIBI) monitoring event, including five Shorecrest High School students working towards their community service goals.  Two teams collected the 12 standard samples on McAleer and Lyon creeks.  OUr data is now getting incorporated into a broader Puget Sound Database which can be found at the Puget Sound Stream Benthos website.
