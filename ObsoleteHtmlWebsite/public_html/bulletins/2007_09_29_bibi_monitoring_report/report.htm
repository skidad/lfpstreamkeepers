BIBI Monitoring Report

September 30 2007  

We had a good turnout for the Fall 2007 Benthic Inventory of Biological Integrity (BIBI) monitoring event. Two teams collected a total of 12 samples on McAleer and Lyon creeks.  We had an enthusiastic Brookside Sixth Grader getting community service credits and a City Councelman making public contact.
