StreamKeepers of Lake Forest Park

Saturday April 16, 2005 Monitoring Report

 April 16, 2005
Despite the pouring rain we had a good turnout of about 10 people Saturday morning at 9AM. We will post the data when we have it collated.

A Question: During the preliminary discussion we had a question come up that we've all heard before: Who do you call when you see something funny in the streams? One of this days streamkeepers has seen the stream in her back yard turn white on several occasions in the past few years, most recently within the past couple of weeks. Our best answer was call Frank Zenk at the City of Lake Forest Park. We would like to have a definitive answer to this question. There are a number of agencies including Washington Department of Ecology, Washington Fish and Wildlife that have some jurisdiction.
