April 14, 2005: Fecal Coliform Sampling

 April 29, 2005
To get a general idea of the bacterial health of our streams we sampled the water in 11 places on Thursday Morning April 14 and sent the samples to the City of Everett lab for fecal coliform testing. The data came back interesting but not alarming. For the report see the data links on this site.
