Saturday January 17, 2009 Monitoring Report

January 17, 2008

We had a turn out of about 10 people for the January 17, 2009 Basic Water Quality monitoring event.  Mark Phillips reported that the city has about $2500 earmarked for supporting the LFP Streamkeepers. There is some thought going towards buying some electronic measurment tools for doing Basic Quality monitoring to augment our current methods. 

The Jan 17 2009 Basic Quality data can be seen at: http://lfpstreamkeepers.org/data/chemical/WaterQlty2009.htm
