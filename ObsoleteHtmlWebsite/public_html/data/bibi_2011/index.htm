Sampling Date: September 17, 2011
Sample Collection: Lake Forest Park Stream Keepers
Analysis: King County Department of Natural Resources and Parks

We used a different protocol for collection this time around, working with Jo Wilhelm at King County Department of Natural Resources to help with their effort to determine if the three replicate system we have been using in past years can be correlated to the 8 replicate system that King County is using. The data posted here contains a number of other sites around King County allowing the comparison of McAleer creek with other King County urban streams. The overall 24 rating of McAleer is at the top end of poor, as we understand it. This data has been incorporated into the Puget Sound Stream Benthos data management system (www.pugetsoundstreambenthos.org).
